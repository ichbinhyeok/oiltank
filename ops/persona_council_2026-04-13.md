# Persona Council - 2026-04-13

## Room
- Demand realist
- SERP realist
- Funnel operator
- Risk operator
- Sponsor seller
- Judge

## Forced question
If a solo founder can only start one new high-intent site after `radonverdict`, should a buried-oil-tank decision engine be built now?

## Demand realist
- Yes.
- The user state is hot:
  - under contract
  - seller de-risking a listing
  - records missing
  - tank suspected
  - leak concern
- These are not casual readers.

## SERP realist
- Yes, with scope discipline.
- The winning surface is not broad `oil tank removal`.
- The winning surface is state and scenario specific.
- Official plus local-specialist SERPs leave room for a new domain that is sharper than both.

## Funnel operator
- Yes.
- The CTA stack is unusually clean:
  - verify with sweep
  - route to closure or removal
  - escalate to cleanup if needed
- This can stay form-first and email-first.

## Risk operator
- Yes, but only if the site never pretends to diagnose a property.
- The biggest danger is acting like a certainty engine.
- The correct tone is:
  - likely
  - verify
  - in this state
  - based on the available record

## Sponsor seller
- Yes.
- This is attractive because the partner types are obvious and high-value:
  - tank locate
  - certified closure
  - remediation
- The site does not need giant traffic if lead quality is sharp.

## Judge
- Build now.
- Confidence: `8.5 / 10`

## The winning one-sentence product definition
Build a state-first post-trigger decision engine for buyers, sellers, and homeowners dealing with a suspected, confirmed, abandoned, or leaking residential heating-oil tank.

## The biggest trap
Do not widen into generic heating-oil, broad environmental anxiety, or soft home-improvement SEO.

## Product synthesis - 2026-04-14
- Demand realist is now reflected in the home surface through explicit hot-state language:
  - under contract
  - seller de-risking
  - missing records
  - suspected tank
  - leak concern
- Funnel operator is now reflected in a visible decision path:
  - verify first
  - route by evidence
  - negotiate or escalate only when the evidence earns it
- Risk operator is now reflected in explicit case separation on the front door:
  - suspected tank
  - confirmed tank
  - leak concern
- SERP realist remains reflected in scope discipline:
  - public front door stays state-first and scenario-first
  - advanced support routes stay contextual instead of becoming broad navigation
- Sponsor seller remains reflected in partner order:
  - sweep or locate first
  - closure or removal second
  - cleanup escalation third
